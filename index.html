Welcome!

Hello and welcome to Macrae Web! We are a UK-based web designer, developer and hosting provider. We can design and create your website, and then host the website using our eco-friendly server provider. Whether you’re looking for a website to advertise your small business, a personal site to show off your hobby, or an ecommerce site to sell your goods, we’ve got the knowledge and skills to bring that vision to life.

Design

Whatever type of website you’re looking for we can design it to fit your needs. Whether that’s showcasing your business services, operating your online retail presence, or a personal website.

Build

We will build your website using all the tools at our disposal. We will also build your website to look great on any device, from large screen PC’s to the phone in your pocket.

Host

Together with our eco-friendly hosting provider we will keep your website live and available to all of your customers, friends and family, without you having to do any maintenance yourselves.
