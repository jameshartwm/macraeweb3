The world has gone mobile

An ever increasing number of people use their mobile phones to access websites, in fact an estimated 54% of website traffic is now on mobile, up from 31% in 2015! (source) This trend is why at Macrae Web we don't just make sure your website looks and functions great on laptop or desktop computers, but also on mobile and tablet devices too. This approach is called responsive web design, and gives you the peace of mind that your website visitors are getting a great, easy to navigate, experience however they choose to browse the web.
