Welcome!

Find out how Macrae Web can help your business excel online

Hello and welcome to Macrae Web! We are a UK-based web designer, developer and hosting provider, situated in the heart of picturesque Warwickshire. We can design and create your website, and then host the website using our eco-friendly server provider. Whether you’re looking for a website to advertise your small business, a personal site to show off your hobby, or an ecommerce site to sell your goods, we’ve got the knowledge and skills to bring that vision to life.

3 steps to your new website

1. Design

Whatever type of website you’re looking for we can design it to fit your needs. Whether that’s showcasing your business services, operating your online retail presence, or a personal website.

2. Build

We will build your website using all the tools at our disposal. We will also build your website to look great on any device, from large screen PC’s to the phone in your pocket.

3. Host

Together with our eco-friendly hosting provider we will keep your website live and available to all of your customers, friends and family, without you having to do any maintenance yourselves.

The world has gone mobile

An ever increasing number of people use their mobile phones to access websites, in fact an estimated 54% of website traffic is now on mobile, up from 31% in 2015! This trend is why at Macrae Web we don't just make sure your website looks and functions great on laptop or desktop computers, but also on mobile and tablet devices too. This approach is called responsive web design, and gives you the peace of mind that your website visitors are getting a great, easy to navigate, experience however they choose to browse the web.
