Let's talk money

Websites can vary massively, from a simple one-page website advertising your business, to a multi-page news blog, all the way to massive juggernauts like facebook. That's why it's so difficult to answer the question "how much does a website cost". With that in mind we have covered the most common scenarios in our pricing structure below. We will always quote you up front on any work we do, so there are no surprises.

Design & Build

For a basic up single page website, with contact information, your images and your text the charge would be £195. For a multi-page website (similar to this one!) the cost would be £295. For bigger projects with custom forms, ecommerce facilities etc. the price would be quoted fully up front. We will work with you to tailor the site to your needs and expectations.

from £195

Host

We can host your website, including a free domain name (website address) if you don’t already have one. This is priced at £12.95 per month, however for high-traffic websites, or sites with large media or data transfers, this may increase. You will always be quoted up front if the price differs.

from £12.95 per month
